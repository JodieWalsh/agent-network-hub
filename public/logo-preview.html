Option 1: Key + Network (Horizontal)
Key symbol representing buyer access and advocacy, connected to a network of collaboration points. Emphasizes the "unlocking" role buyers agents play and their professional connections.

Option 2: BA Monogram Badge
"BA" lettermark representing Buyers Agent in a professional badge design. Modern and minimal, works perfectly as an icon while maintaining brand identity.
Best for: Favicon, app icon, social media profile
Option 3: Collaboration Network
Buyers agents (represented as people holding keys) connected in a network around the buyer's property. Visually shows collaboration, advocacy, and the collective expertise of the platform.
Best for: Marketing materials, presentation slides, about page
Option 4: Elegant Wordmark
Clean typography with subtle key accent above and connected underline below. Tagline emphasizes core values: buyer advocacy, collaboration, and expertise.
Best for: Email signatures, documents, simple headers
